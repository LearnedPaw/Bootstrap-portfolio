About Me

I go by Learned Paw on many of my social accounts. But, my name is Blessing. I am an accidential coder/developer. I did a stint in SEO content article writing and looked for something I could do to take a break from 500 word articles about yacht rentals, new products that were all about the "wow" factor, rentals, etc, and any other topics you can imagine. I learned how to code to make my day more profitable in doing all sorts of online tasks. From categorization of pictures to assisting with machine learning. My strength is looking at workflow and imagining how to reduce the time spent by at least 50%. In learning how to do this, I fell in love with the magic of what is possible to automate and do it on a screen that has my favortie colors and font size. Being able to code makes me feel like I am living in a world just as magical and full of opportunity as "Harry Potter." I look forward to an opportunity to being a part of your dream project and helping make it a reality. Anything is possible and I want to be a part of it.
